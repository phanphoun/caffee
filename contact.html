Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Are your coffee beans ethically sourced?

Absolutely! All our coffee beans are 100% ethically sourced and come with certifications from fair trade organizations and sustainability programs.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all unopened coffee products. Items must be in original condition with all packaging intact.

Do you provide coffee grinding services?

Yes, we offer complimentary grinding services for all coffee beans. You can specify your preferred grind size during checkout or contact us for custom requests.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order status in your account dashboard.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, we offer complimentary gift wrapping on all orders. You can also add a personalized message during checkout.
